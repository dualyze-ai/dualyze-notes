# Spaghetti Carbonara

> Draft — needs review before publishing.

Working notes for the Carbonara recipe.

## Ingredients

- Spaghetti (400g)
- Guanciale or pancetta (200g)
- Eggs — 2 whole eggs plus 2 extra yolks
- Pecorino Romano, finely grated (or a mix with Parmigiano Reggiano)
- Black pepper, freshly ground
- Salted water for cooking pasta

## Method

1. Cook spaghetti in well-salted boiling water until al dente.
2. Cut guanciale into cubes. Fry in a dry pan over medium heat until crispy.
3. Whisk together eggs, grated Pecorino Romano, and a generous amount of black pepper.
4. Reserve a ladle of starchy pasta cooking water before draining.
5. Off the heat, add the drained spaghetti to the pan with guanciale.
6. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the spaghetti. Toss quickly.
7. Add pasta cooking water a little at a time until the sauce reaches a creamy consistency.
8. Serve immediately with extra Pecorino Romano and black pepper.

## Tips

- Some cooks use rigatoni instead of spaghetti.
- A mix of Pecorino Romano and Parmigiano Reggiano gives a milder flavour.
- Add a small splash of pasta water to the egg mixture beforehand to temper the eggs.
