# Kyoto Day Trip

Notes from a single-day visit to Kyoto from Tokyo.

## Overview

- Single day, no overnight stay
- Departed Tokyo 7:00 / Returned 21:00

## Itinerary

- 09:30: Fushimi Inari — walked up to the mid-point
- 13:00: Gion district — afternoon stroll
- 15:00: Nishiki Market — lunch and snacks
- 17:00: Shinkansen back to Tokyo

## Transportation

- Shinkansen from Tokyo (2h 15min each way)
- IC card (Suica) for local buses in Kyoto

## Budget

- Transport: ¥28,000 (round trip Shinkansen)
- Food: ¥3,000
- Admission: ¥0
- Total: ¥31,000

## Tips

- Not enough time for everything — consider a 2-night stay next time
- Early start is essential for Fushimi Inari
- Get Suica card at the airport
