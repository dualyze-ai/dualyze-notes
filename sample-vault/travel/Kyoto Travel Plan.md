# Kyoto Travel Plan

A 2-day trip to Kyoto, Japan. Temples, shrines, and traditional streets.

## Overview

- Dates: October 2024
- Duration: 2 nights
- Base: Kyoto Station area

## Itinerary

- Day 1: Fushimi Inari, Gion, Nishiki Market
- Day 2: Arashiyama, Kinkakuji, Nijo Castle

## Accommodations

- Cross Hotel Kyoto (2 nights)
- Check-in: Oct 20 / Check-out: Oct 22

## Transportation

- IC card (Suica) for local buses and trains
- Shinkansen from Osaka: JR Pass
- Bicycle rental for Arashiyama

## Budget

- Accommodation: ¥40,000
- Food: ¥20,000
- Transport: ¥10,000
- Activities: ¥8,000
- Total: ¥78,000

## Tips

- Fushimi Inari is best visited early morning
- Arashiyama bamboo grove is crowded by midday
- Gion is busiest on weekends — visit on weekdays
