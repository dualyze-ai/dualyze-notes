# Osaka Travel Plan

A 3-day trip to Osaka, Japan. Food, castles, and day trips to Nara.

## Overview

- Dates: October 2024
- Duration: 3 nights
- Base: Namba

## Itinerary

- Day 1: Arrival, Dotonbori, Namba walk
- Day 2: Day trip to Nara
- Day 3: Osaka Castle, Shinsekai, departure

## Accommodations

- Dormy Inn Namba (3 nights)
- Check-in: Oct 17 / Check-out: Oct 20

## Transportation

- IC card (Suica or ICOCA) for local trains and buses
- Day trip to Nara: Kintetsu Nara Line
- Shinkansen from Tokyo: JR Pass

## Budget

- Accommodation: ¥45,000
- Food: ¥25,000
- Transport: ¥15,000
- Activities: ¥10,000
- Total: ¥95,000

## Tips

- Dotonbori at night is a must-see
- Book Nara deer park visit for morning
- Osaka Card for discount on major attractions
