# Seoul Travel Notes

Rough notes from a trip to Seoul, South Korea.

## Highlights

- Gyeongbokgung Palace
- Hongdae nightlife
- Myeongdong shopping
- Korean BBQ near Mapo

## Getting There

- Direct flight from Narita (2h 30min)
- AREX train from Incheon Airport to city centre

## Food

- Samgyeopsal at a local BBQ spot
- Bibimbap at Gwangjang Market
- Hotteok from street stalls in Insadong

## Budget

- Flights: ¥60,000
- Accommodation: ¥40,000
- Food: ¥15,000
- Total: ¥115,000

## Notes

- T-money card for subway and buses
- Naver Maps works better than Google Maps in Korea
- Bring cash for street food stalls
