# Tokyo Travel Plan

> Draft — review before finalising.

A 5-day trip to Tokyo, Japan. Covering key neighbourhoods, food, and day trips.

## Overview

- Dates: October 2024
- Duration: 5 nights
- Base: Shinjuku

## Itinerary

- Day 1: Arrival, Shinjuku walk
- Day 2: Shibuya, Harajuku, Omotesando
- Day 3: Asakusa, Ueno, Akihabara
- Day 4: Day trip to Nikko or Kamakura (TBD)
- Day 5: Ginza, departure

## Accommodations

- Hotel Gracery Shinjuku (4 nights) — confirm booking
- Alternative: Keio Plaza Hotel

## Transportation

- JR Pass (7-day) for bullet trains and JR lines
- IC card (Suica) for local trains
- Narita Express (N'EX) from the airport

## Budget

- Flights: ¥120,000
- Accommodation: ¥80,000
- Food: ¥25,000
- Transport: ¥25,000
- Activities: ¥15,000
- Total: ¥265,000

## Tips

- Book Nikko tickets in advance
- Get Suica card at the airport
- Shinjuku station is large — allow extra navigation time
